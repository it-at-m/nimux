Welcome to NIMux Home

NIMux is a smart app that centrally manages supplies, inventory, materials, and extras. Keep track of all your stock, work materials, and resources at any time – ideal for emergency supplies, warehouse management, or daily operations.

Features

  • Easy creation, categorization, and management of items
  • Real-time overview of stock levels, reservations, and usage
  • Centralized organization of all resources
  • Smart extras: statistics

NIMux – Everything under control, intelligently organized.
